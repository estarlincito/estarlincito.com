<Contents>

To truly thrive, you must give yourself the love you've been seeking from others—this is the essence of self-love.

If you have experienced feelings of abandonment, it's essential to stop searching for affection outside of yourself. The love and validation you need are already within you—waiting to be embraced.

Release the anxiety of begging for small scraps of affection from others. Don’t let people into your life who only approach you for their own benefit—unless they are paying for your services. Instead, surround yourself with those who genuinely care for you, add value to your life, and offer something real and meaningful.
