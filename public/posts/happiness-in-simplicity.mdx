<Intruduction>

> True happiness lies in self-sufficiency and in appreciating what you already have.

</Intruduction>

<Contents>

_The satisfaction you feel when you do things for yourself is immense: working to earn your own money, then buying what you want._

_Cooking the food you’re going to eat, cleaning the house, organizing everything—when you see the results, you feel that indescribable satisfaction._

_You feel that everything is going well in your life, even though there may be things you’d prefer. But know that those things are not more important than the very act of living._

_So in the end, you learn to enjoy the things you have now, because if you’re not happy with what you have, you won’t be happy with what you think you need. That’s called personal satisfaction._ 🧸

</Contents>
